##### Abstract

The "dam en list" refers to sections of administrative tablets that mention female members of the Ebla royal family. These women are listed alongside other court members as recipients of garments and precious objects. Since Tonietti's study in 1989, three new complete lists of dam en have been published, with additional fragmentary lists discovered later. In this note, I provide an updated index of the dam en lists and offer general remarks to aid non-specialist readers in understanding the material.
