About this graph

This graph is a visual representation of the data published in Archi, Alfonso (2000), "The 'Lords', lugal-lugal, of Ebla: A Prosopographic Study" Vicino Oriente 12, 19-59 (link). Nodes represent the proper names of the lugals or the mu-DU documents in which their appear according to the chronology established by Archi 2000: whenever a lord is mentioned, he is connected to the text by a link.
