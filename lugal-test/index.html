The Prosopography of the lugals

A Network Study

This graph is a visual representation of the data published in Archi, Alfonso (2000), “The ‘Lords’, lugal-lugal, of Ebla. A Prosopographic Study.” Vicino Oriente 12: 19–59. Nodes represent the lugals and the mu-DU documents in which their appear: whenever a lord is mentioned, he is connected to the text by a link.
